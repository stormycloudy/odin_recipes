Casserole Recipe

The casserole: Cilantro, bell peppers, bacon, and cheese pack this colorful casserole. Serve with sliced avocado, sour cream, and fresh salsa on the side. You could substitute Mexican chorizo sausage for the bacon for a spicier version.

Ingredients

  • cooking spay
  • 1 package bacon
  • 10 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 medium red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels. Crumble bacon into a bowl.
  3. Beat eggs and milk together in a bowl; pour into the prepared baking dish.
  4. Layer bacon, red bell pepper, onion, cilantro, and Swiss cheese over the egg mixture.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are set, about 30 minutes.
